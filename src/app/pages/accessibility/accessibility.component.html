Accessibility statement for Stockify

We want everyone who visits the Stockify website to feel welcome and find the experience rewarding.

What are we doing?

To help us make the Stockify website a positive place for everyone, we've been using the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities, and user friendly for everyone.

The guidelines have three levels of accessibility (A, AA and AAA). We've chosen Level A as the target for the Stockify website.
