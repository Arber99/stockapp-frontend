1. What is a stock?

A stock is a piece of a company. If you own a stock, then you own a piece of that company. The more stocks you buy, the larger the percentage is your ownage of that company.

Example: Let's say for a company called "XYZ" there exist a total of 100 stocks. If you own 7 stocks, then you own a total of 7% of company XYZ.
